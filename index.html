About this dashboard

This Student Fee Management Dashboard is designed for teachers who want a simple way to manage student records and monthly fee payments on laptop or phone. It helps store student details such as name, class, roll number, contact information, admission date, and monthly fee data.

Main features

  • Add and save student details with fee information
  • Track monthly payments from January to December
  • View total students, paid months and total fees collected
  • Store records in the browser for quick access later
  • Use the dashboard on desktop and mobile browsers
